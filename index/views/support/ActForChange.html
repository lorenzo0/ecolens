EPFL ActForChange lab

Since January 2021, EcoLens has been supported by the Act4Change LAB at the EPFL. Act4Change LAB is a program from EPFL to support innovative and sustainable projects imagined by students or collaborators for the campus. A financial and advisory help is provided to the selected projects. The application fields vary from alimentation, energy, mobility to waste management, climate, social interactions and more. https://www.epfl.ch/about/sustainability/fr/communaute-a4c/act4changelab/
