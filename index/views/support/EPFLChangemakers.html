EPFL Changemarkers

In the 2020 fall semester, EcoLens cofounder Alessandra Capurro took part to the EPFL Changemaker program.

During 12 weeks, 20 EPFL students with great ideas were attending lessons and workshops to improve their entrepreneurship, sustainable and leadership skills. At the end of the program, Alessandra was proud to win two awards for the Best pitch and Best sustainable project for EcoLens.

Even to this day, the Changemaker program still provides us with valuable advice, contacts and opportunities.
